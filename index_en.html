Why Support This Project?

Smartphone Requests

Customers no longer need to crowd around songbooks. Just send them the link, they choose the track, and the unified queue on your dashboard organizes everything automatically.

Direct Download (YouTube)

Did someone request that brand new track you don't have on your drive? No panic! Search and download new songs straight from YouTube in seconds, ready to hit the stage.

Automatic Break DJ

While the crowd catches their breath, the system takes over with interval music. Switch between Karaoke and Break with a single click, without missing a beat.

Fast Manual Requests

For those who still prefer writing on paper, the operator can add multiple singers to the queue at once using quick keyboard shortcuts.

Unified Intuitive Queue

Organization Made Simple

Never lose track of the singer order. The main list clearly displays who is next, separating the playlist from local media and allowing you to reorder live with just two clicks.

The Dark Mode design rests your eyes and focuses on the main attraction: the vibrant name of the next star hitting the stage.

Remote Display Screen
