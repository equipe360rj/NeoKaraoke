Features

These are some of the main features of NeoKaraoke Professional

Independent Singer Queue

Management of a waiting list for people, separate from the video playback queue.

Real-time Smart Search

Instant location of songs by name or partial term in all configured folders.

Full Queue Control

Buttons to move up, move down, restart, or remove songs from the playback queue with one click.

Automatic Background Music

System that fills the silence between karaoke performances with interval playlists.

Crossfade Effect (DJ Style)

Smooth transitions between interval songs, preventing sharp cuts between tracks.

Smart Fade Out/In

Gradually lowers the break volume when starting a video and when returning to the interval.

Integrated Streaming and Download

Browser to search for online videos with the option of direct playback via streaming in VLC or downloading to the library.

Web Panel with Dynamic Display*

Control of the public panel (Queue, Slideshow, Advertising, "Time to Sing", and "Last Call").

Online Catalog via QR Code*

Automatic receipt of requests made by the public via QR Code/Mobile.

Sound Effects System (FX)*

4 buttons for instant audio triggering for sound effects (applause, sirens, etc.).

Real-time catalog update*

Automatic synchronization of the song catalog with your website whenever there are changes.

* Only in the PRO version
